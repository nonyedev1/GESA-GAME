How The Game Works

It tests how good the player is at guessing. It generates random numbers from 1-20 and prompts the player to guess the random number. At the start of the game, there is a default 20 points. At every wrong guess, the player loses 1 point. at 0 point the player loses the game, and with a correct guess the player wins and the score is saved.
